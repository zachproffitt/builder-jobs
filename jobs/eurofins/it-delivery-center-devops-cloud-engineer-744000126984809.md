As a team member you will act as the liaison between cloud architects and regional and local IT teams as well as local business stakeholders. Based on the knowledge, the team will help application teams modernize their applications to suit cloud-based deployment and modern authentication.

How can you help us?

The main objectives are:

Design and implement cloud-based solutions on Azure, AWS & OpenStack

Mentor, Manage and optimize multiple Kubernetes clusters (AKS, EKS, Rancher)

Implement and maintain the Terraform- based release and configuration management solution.

Idempotent configuration management using PowerShell Desired State Configuration (DSC)

Collaborate, consult, train and support different IT teams to adopt and implement the new standards and ways of working.

Help various IT teams cover major incidents

Examples of tasks:

Contact regional IT team, collect requirements and implement standard operating procedures.

Deploy an application landing zone in your Azure or AWS environment using the Terraform and Azure DevOps based framework.

GitOps approach to solving technical problems.

Advise application teams on Cloud Adoption patterns and advice.

Serving as an escalation point for all IT teams for support and troubleshooting,

Provide quality documentation in an automated fashion, facilitating understanding and knowledge sharing.

Collaborate closely with the rest of the IT infrastructure and operations, architecture, application and security teams.

Identify opportunities to innovate, automate, and simplify delivery by contributing Terraform code and developing modules based on common patterns.

What can we do for you?

Benefits:

We offer the following:

A competitive salary package including bonuses and benefits

Extensive training and on-the-job training opportunities

Challenges within a multicultural and cross-regional team as part of the international and fast-growing organization Eurofins

A dynamic work environment with a good team spirit, where personal development and growth are highly encouraged
