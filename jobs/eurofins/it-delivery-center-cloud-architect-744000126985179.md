In a nutshell

As a Cloud/Devops Architect at Eurofins, you will be supporting our global IT Application development teams to move towards a cloud-first and DevOps mind-set and transition many of our applications to be deployed, run and operated via automation in the public cloud.

Where will you be based and what business will you support?

You will be part of a geographically dispersed IT Architecture team with a focus on supporting our global IT Application development teams.

How can you help us?

You will collaborate with the Cloud Engineering Team in defining cloud standards and governance, and with Product Development to define their technology transition into the cloud.

You will work on automation of processes, testing and security; and on document standards in readiness for governance approval.

You will design scalable enterprise cloud frameworks (AWS control Tower, Azure Landing Zones etc), define and standardise tooling; and improve software performance metrics.

You will be a trusted hands-on advisor for IaC and CI/CD pipelines, while evangelising DevOps/Cloud culture and practices; and consulting with business to build new channels between development and operations teams.
