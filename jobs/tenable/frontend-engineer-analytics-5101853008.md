Who is Tenable?

Tenable® is the Exposure Management company. 44,000 organizations around the globe rely on Tenable to understand and reduce cyber risk. Our global employees support 65 percent of the Fortune 500, 45 percent of the Global 2000, and large government agencies. Come be part of our journey!

What makes Tenable such a great place to work?

Ask a member of our team and they’ll answer, “Our people!” We work together to build and innovate best-in-class cybersecurity solutions for our customers; all while creating a culture of belonging, respect, and excellence where we can be our best selves. When you’re part of our #OneTenable team, you can expect to partner with some of the most talented and passionate people in the industry, and have the support and resources you need to do work that truly matters. We deliver results that exceed expectations and we win together!

Your Role:

As a Frontend Engineer on our team, you will be a key contributor to the evolution of our product platform. We are looking for a developer who thrives on the challenge of building from the ground up - someone who is equally comfortable helping to define a new technical foundation as they are diving into the day-to-day work of feature development and ticket execution.

You will work as a core member of an agile team, partnering closely with Product and Design to translate requirements into high-performance, user-centric interfaces. This role is ideal for a proactive contributor who is energized by the end-to-end development process—from shaping architectural foundations to delivering on daily product goals—ensuring we provide a seamless experience for our customers while maintaining the stability of our current production environment.

What You’ll do:

Build and maintain modern frontend infrastructure, ensuring it is scalable, performant, and easy to extend.

Take full ownership of features from design to deployment, including handling day-to-day development tasks and support tickets to keep the product moving forward.

Collaborate cross-functionally with product, design, and backend teams to build intuitive and high-performance user interfaces.

Establish best practices for code quality, component reusability, and testing.

Collaborate with backend engineers to ensure smooth data flow and efficient API usage as our services evolve.

What You'll Need:

3+ years of experience in frontend development with a strong focus on building modern, responsive web applications.

Deep proficiency in TypeScript and experience with modern frameworks (such as Angular or React).

Strong autodidactic abilities with the flexibility to move between different frameworks and technologies as the project evolves.

A solid understanding of UI/UX principles and a passion for delivering a polished final product for the user.

Experience with unit testing, code reviews, and maintaining high standards in a production-ready environment.

Bonus: Proven experience in companies dealing with Security SaaS B2B.
