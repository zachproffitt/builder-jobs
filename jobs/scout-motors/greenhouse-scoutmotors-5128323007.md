What you’ll do

Become part of an iconic brand that is set to revolutionize the electric pick-up truck &amp; rugged SUV marketplace by achieving the following:

Help design, develop, maintain, and release our iOS applications.&nbsp;

Drive the technical architecture and implementation of mobile&nbsp;platform&nbsp;and solutions from the ground up, ensuring they are scalable, reliable, and performant.&nbsp;

Grow the knowledge of the team, sharing through pair programming, code reviews, presentations, example code, and documentation.&nbsp;

Implement programming best practices, deployment processes and monitoring for mobile platforms.&nbsp;

Stay current with emerging technologies and industry trends in mobile development, advocating for the adoption of new tools and techniques as appropriate.&nbsp;

Location &amp; Travel Expectations:

This role may be based remotely within the United States. This role is not eligible for remote work in New York City.

Applicants&nbsp;should expect that the role&nbsp;may&nbsp;require the ability to convene with Scout colleagues in person and be able to travel to participate in events on behalf of the company&nbsp;from time to time.

What you’ll bring&nbsp;

We expect all Scout employees to have integrity, curiosity, resourcefulness, and strive to exhibit a positive attitude, as well as a growth mindset. You’ll be comfortable with change and flexible in a fast-paced, high-growth environment. You’ll take a collaborative approach to achieve ambitious goals. Here's what else you'll bring:&nbsp;

Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field.&nbsp;

5+ years of hands-on experience in software engineering shipping customer facing applications on iOS.&nbsp;

Proficiency in Swift&nbsp;and SwiftUI&nbsp;

Solid understanding of mobile platform architecture, UI/UX design principles and frameworks, GraphQL, mobile security and performance optimization techniques.&nbsp;&nbsp;

Experience with version control systems (e.g., Git), CI/CD pipelines&nbsp;for mobile apps,&nbsp;and mobile release processes.&nbsp;

Excellent problem-solving skills and a strong attention to detail.&nbsp;

Effective communication skills, with the ability to articulate technical concepts to both technical and non-technical stakeholders.&nbsp;

Experience operating in start-up settings, launching 0 to 1 product, and driving graduation to highly customized applications.&nbsp;

Ability to effectively manage multiple priorities and stakeholders in a fast-paced, dynamic environment.&nbsp;

Passion for mobile technology and a commitment to delivering exceptional user experiences.&nbsp;

Preferred Qualifications:&nbsp;&nbsp;

Experience building systems in the automotive industry, or experience working on IoT devices&nbsp;

Experience with mobile app testing, experimentation frameworks and automation frameworks.&nbsp;
