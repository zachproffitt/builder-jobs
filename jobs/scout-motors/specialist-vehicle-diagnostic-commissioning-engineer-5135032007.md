What you’ll do

Become part of an iconic brand that is set to revolutionize the electric pick-up truck &amp; rugged SUV marketplace by achieving the following:

Support the integration of a new diagnostic system for electronic vehicle commissioning from concept to production.&nbsp;

Develop the data model for vehicle commissioning via the collection of information to process and create visualizations.&nbsp;&nbsp;

Create dashboards to ensure visibility and communication of the data&nbsp;

Evaluate,&nbsp;analyze&nbsp;and improve the data obtained by monitoring and troubleshooting&nbsp;&nbsp;

Evaluate, recommend, and implement modern data tooling and infrastructure improvements to enhance our analytical capabilities and data platform.&nbsp;&nbsp;

Plan and execute tests and verification steps for the owned systems together with all necessary interfaces end-to-end&nbsp;

Plan, coordinate, and implement equipment and process in the new Car-IT Architecture test system.&nbsp;

Collaborate with cross-functional teams to implement and integrate the commissioning sequences and processes.&nbsp;

Support development of software programs and scripts to execute electronic vehicle commissioning and tests.&nbsp;

Program sequence code on various equipment such as wheel alignment, roller benches, and fluid filling systems in the Universal Testing System.&nbsp;

Troubleshoot and resolve technical issues related to equipment integration and data processing.&nbsp;

Develop short-term solutions/workarounds for production problems.&nbsp;

Identify&nbsp;potential process risks in production processes and develop counter measures to prevent vehicle loss.&nbsp;

Maintain and improve&nbsp;program process&nbsp;sustainability.&nbsp;&nbsp;

Assess the usability of check programs under series production conditions.&nbsp;

Work with cross-functional teams to improve product and process quality.&nbsp;
