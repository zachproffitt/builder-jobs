What You'll Do
We’re looking for a User Interface Programmer who is passionate about games and well-versed in C++, UI development, and mobile development. In this role you will work closely with artists, designers, and other programmers on cutting-edge features that support player experiences across Fortnite.
In this role, you will&nbsp;
Work closely with art and design to ensure that UI meets aesthetic and functional requirements
Attend to every detail, especially with regard to compliance, layout, and design
Optimize and fixing bugs in existing UI systems
Contribute ideas toward all aspects of the game’s production and development
Write robust, maintainable and performant code
What we’re looking for
Firm grasp of C++ / tech fundamentals&nbsp;
Unreal Engine development experience
Profiling and optimization skills for mobile
General experience with web services&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
Passion for games and great user experiences&nbsp;&nbsp;
A keen analytical mind with mature problem-solving skills
Enjoy working with a cross-discipline, geographically distributed team
