Every day, tens of millions of people come to Roblox to explore, create, play, learn, and connect with friends in 3D immersive digital experiences– all created by our global community of developers and creators.&nbsp;

At Roblox, we’re building the tools and platform that empower our community to bring any experience that they can imagine to life. Our vision is to reimagine the way people come together, from anywhere in the world, and on any device. We’re on a mission to connect a billion people with optimism and civility, and looking for amazing talent to help us get there.&nbsp;

A career at Roblox means you’ll be working to shape the future of human interaction, solving unique technical challenges at scale, and helping to create safer, more civil shared experiences for everyone.

The Consumer Platforms - Web &amp; Backend team owns the core web experience for www.roblox.com and the backend services that power Roblox’s consumer apps across mobile, console, desktop, and web. The team provides the foundational architecture, frameworks, and services that other product teams build on, ensuring Roblox is fast, reliable, and delightful for all our users.

As a Senior Fullstack Software Engineer on this team, you’ll work across both the web platform and its backend, shaping the experience where millions of people first discover and return to Roblox. You will balance platform excellence with direct product impact - you will architect and ship end-to-end product features that define how millions of users interact with our ecosystem daily.

&nbsp;

You will:

Drive the full product lifecycle in close collaboration with product and design teams, transforming complex requirements into high-performance, polished features

Lead systematic improvements to our UI development processes and architecture.

Architect, build, and maintain our design system and component library in collaboration with cross-functional teams, while innovating associated developer tooling to ensure consistency, scalability, and boosting team productivity.

Champion best practices for writing clean, maintainable, and well-documented code.

Stay ahead of industry trends in frontend &amp; backend development, component-based frameworks, and UI/UX to drive continuous improvement.

You have:

4+ years of experience as a full-stack engineer, with a proven track record of building and shipping production-level applications.

Expertise in modern frontend technologies and component-based UI frameworks like React.

Expertise in an Object-Oriented Programming language (e.g., C#, Java, C++).

Solid experience with backend development, including designing APIs, working with microservices and databases, and building scalable server-side logic using languages such as Node.js

A deep understanding of the entire application lifecycle, from architecture and design to deployment and maintenance.

A strong design sensibility and a commitment to creating highly accessible and visually polished user interfaces.

Exceptional communication and collaboration skills, with the ability to lead discussions and work effectively with product, design, and other engineering teams.

Experience with any of the following is a plus: NextJS, Node.js, Tailwind CSS, C++, Lua, contributing to or managing a design system.

Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science or equivalent professional experience.
