Every day, tens of millions of people come to Roblox to explore, create, play, learn, and connect with friends in 3D immersive digital experiences– all created by our global community of developers and creators.&nbsp;

At Roblox, we’re building the tools and platform that empower our community to bring any experience that they can imagine to life. Our vision is to reimagine the way people come together, from anywhere in the world, and on any device. We’re on a mission to connect a billion people with optimism and civility, and looking for amazing talent to help us get there.&nbsp;

A career at Roblox means you’ll be working to shape the future of human interaction, solving unique technical challenges at scale, and helping to create safer, more civil shared experiences for everyone.

Be a part of Roblox Corporation as a Senior Security Software Engineer in Vulnerability Management. Join our dedicated team to ensure the security of our platform. You will work on scaling vulnerability management tools across the company, providing millions of users with a flawless and secure experience. This role offers an outstanding opportunity to contribute to global projects.

You Will:

Develop and maintain internal software products and capabilities to drive awareness and resolution of vulnerabilities at scale within Roblox.

Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify, prioritize, and mitigate security vulnerabilities through tools and services.

Design and build automated systems to highlight security issues to owners and make vulnerability remediation a first-class user experience.

Develop tools and features that help make vulnerability remediation understandable and easy across Roblox’s entire tech stack and organization.&nbsp;

Mentor and guide junior engineers in developing secure software and products, incorporating vulnerability management methodologies.

You Have:

Proven experience in software engineering, ideally with a focus on security.

Proficiency in programming languages such as Python, Go, Java, or C#.

Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.

A track record of delivering features and learning new technologies.

Experience solving operational problems with software-based solutions.

You Are:

A software engineer with 4+ years of experience in working in security or related fields.

Passionate about security and dedicated to crafting secure online experiences.

An outstanding communicator who effectively conveys complex technical concepts to diverse audiences.

Highly collaborative, with a proven ability to work effectively in a team-oriented environment.

Ambitious and driven to stay up-to-date on emerging technologies and security innovations.
