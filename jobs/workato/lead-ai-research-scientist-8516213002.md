Responsibilities

Workato’s AI Research Lab is seeking an exceptional Lead AI Research Scientist to join our growing team and define the next generation of enterprise AI systems.

This is a research-first role focused on redefining and advancing enterprise AI, with an emphasis on real-world impact for developers. In this role, you will set and execute a 24-month research vision spanning deterministic planners, self-healing automations, retrieval-augmented workflow graphs, and agent evaluation frameworks, while ensuring these innovations translate into real-world impact.

The ideal candidate brings deep technical expertise alongside a strong record of execution. You will build, lead, and mentor a team of applied researchers and interns, fostering a culture of technical excellence, strong authorship, and open scientific contribution.

This role will define and drive forward-looking research initiatives while bridging the gap between cutting-edge innovation and production-ready enterprise solutions. You will partner closely with Product and Engineering teams to productionize prototypes within six months of proof of concept and engage directly with lighthouse customers to validate ideas and convert insights into scalable reference architectures.

In this role, you will also be responsible to:

Defining a 24-month research roadmap focused on automated agent building and optimization. goal based agent deign, synthetic data, automated postraining and RL, eval techniques, model optimizaiton

Hiring, coaching, and inspiring a team of 10+ applied researchers and interns; fostering a publication-quality bar and culture of open science.

Publish peer-reviewed papers and secure patents that differentiate Workato in enterprise AI.

Partner with Product and Engineering to productionize prototypes within six months of proof of concept.

Engage lighthouse customers to validate research and convert learnings into reference architectures.

Requirements

Qualifications / Experience / Technical Skills

MS/PhD in Computer Science, Machine Learning, or a related field.

5+ years leading applied research teams.

Hands-on experience with PyTorch or JAX and modern LLM frameworks.

Proven track record of transitioning research into production within large-scale systems.

Strong publication record in top-tier ML venues (NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR).

Hands-on experience with large-scale model training, transformer architectures, reinforcement learning techniques, and CUDA.

Deep expertise across structured and unstructured enterprise data modalities.

This role is onsite in our SF Office

Clear communicator able to translate complex research into business value.

Comfortable making decisions in a fast-moving, on-site lab environment.

Collaborative mindset; thrives at the intersection of research and product.

Experience operating in high-growth startup environments.
