ABOUT US

 - At Sierra, we’re creating a platform to help businesses build better, more human customer experiences with AI. We are primarily an in-person company based in San Francisco, with growing offices in Atlanta, New York, London, Paris, Madrid, Munich, Singapore, Japan, and Sydney.

 - We are guided by a set of values that are at the core of our actions and define our culture: Trust, Customer Obsession, Craftsmanship, Intensity, and Family. These values are the foundation of our work, and we are committed to upholding them in everything we do.

 - Our co-founders are Bret Taylor https://www.linkedin.com/in/brettaylor/ and Clay Bavor https://www.linkedin.com/in/claybavor/. Bret currently serves as Board Chair of OpenAI. Previously, he was co-CEO of Salesforce (which had acquired the company he founded, Quip) and CTO of Facebook. Bret was also one of Google's earliest product managers and co-creator of Google Maps. Before founding Sierra, Clay spent 18 years at Google, where he most recently led Google Labs. Earlier, he started and led Google’s AR/VR effort, Project Starline, and Google Lens. Before that, Clay led the product and design teams for Google Workspace.

WHAT YOU'LL DO

 - Design and implement Go-to-Market software for our marketing, sales, and agent development teams

 - Architect scalable, reliable integrations between Salesforce and key platforms including Marketing Automation, Configure-Price-Quote, Quote to Cash, Contract Lifecycle Management, ETL, Enrichment, and Content Management platforms

 - Oversee the rollout of major feature updates across GTM platforms, ensuring proper enablement and adoption

 - Own business system platform health by monitoring data syncs between Salesforce and backend systems; proactively troubleshoot issues and outages

 - Guide and uphold development best practices to ensure high-quality code, stable integrations, and a strong foundation for future scale

 - Serve as a bridge between technical and non-technical stakeholders—translating complex requirements into elegant, usable systems

 - Support broader GTM Operations team through thoughtful system design, UX enhancements, and AI-first process optimization

WHAT YOU'LL BRING

 - 4+ years of Salesforce administration and configuration experience (Flow, SOQL, lightning apps, APIs, profiles, permissions, objects, etc.)

 - Experience configuring and managing GTM adjacent tools (e.g. Marketing Automation, Configure-Price-Quote, Quote to Cash, Contract Lifecycle Management, Data Management/Enrichment, Content Management System)

 - Strong SQL skills with ability to perform deep data analysis and build reliable pipelines on ETL platforms (e.g. Reverse ETL platforms / Composable CDP)

 - 4+ years of operational experience supporting Marketing, Sales, and/or Customer Success teams in a fast-paced startup environment

 - Deep understanding of Salesforce platform architecture

 - Ability to clearly articulate technical ideas across diverse audiences—from engineers to sales reps

 - Strong collaboration and project ownership mindset, with a track record of delivering complex systems on time

EVEN BETTER…

 - Built applications using LLMs, ideally with exposure to production-grade AI/ML systems

 - Developed custom Salesforce solutions using apex classes, apex triggers, LWC, SLDS

 - Integrated salesforce with other business systems using REST/SOAP APIs, Platform Events, and External Services

 - Craftsmanship: We get the details right, from the words on the page to the system architecture. We have good taste. When we notice something isn’t right, we take the time to fix it. We are proud of the products we produce. We continuously self-reflect to continuously self-improve.
