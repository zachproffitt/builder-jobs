At Rockstar Games, we create world-class entertainment experiences.&nbsp;

Become part of a team working on some of the most rewarding, large-scale creative projects to be found in any entertainment medium - all within an inclusive, highly-motivated environment where you can learn and collaborate with some of the most talented people in the industry.

Rockstar New York is on the lookout for a passionate Full Stack Engineer who considers software development to be a craft and&nbsp;possesses&nbsp;a passion for tackling complex problems. In this role, you will be part of an enabling team, working across product domains within&nbsp;our Online Services&nbsp;Team.&nbsp;&nbsp;

This is a full-time, in-office position based out of Rockstar’s NYC headquarters in Downtown Manhattan.&nbsp;

WHAT WE DO

The Rockstar Games Online Services Team is a passionate development group, focused on growing and&nbsp;maintaining&nbsp;the technology platform that powers Rockstar’s online titles and our ability to deliver world-class player experiences.&nbsp;&nbsp;

We create connected experiences that are found across our game franchises.&nbsp;&nbsp;

We create online services for our games, including matchmaking, cloud saves, player stats, leaderboards, and more.&nbsp;&nbsp;

The Core Tech: Online Tooling team&nbsp;is responsible for&nbsp;the architecture, foundational systems, and developing supported pathways to enable teams to create and&nbsp;maintain&nbsp;tooling to manage our online environment.&nbsp;

RESPONSIBILITIES

Develop features and applications&nbsp;utilizing&nbsp;C#, MSSQL, and&nbsp;JavaScript/TypeScript&nbsp;with React.&nbsp;

Utilize best practice to ensure full test coverage and&nbsp;contribute to&nbsp;technical documentation.&nbsp;

Collaborate with other teams across our global organization to implement&nbsp;solutions that enhance developer experience.&nbsp;

REQUIREMENTS

A degree in computer science or 3+ years of equivalent experience in professional web development.&nbsp;

3+ years of experience using React (TypeScript/JavaScript).&nbsp;

3+ years of experience using C#.&nbsp;

2+ years of experience with T-SQL.&nbsp;

2+ years of experience with source control, preferably Perforce.&nbsp;

Application of software development best practices.

Knowledge of Agile software development processes.

Great communication skills as a core member of an internationally-based team.&nbsp;

PLUSES

Please note that these are desirable skills and are not required to apply for the position.

Prior experience with Redis,&nbsp;MongoDB&nbsp;and automated testing.&nbsp;

Exposure to Elasticsearch&nbsp;is&nbsp;desirable.&nbsp;

Passion for web technologies and a desire to remain up to date with the latest technologies.&nbsp;

Knowledge and application of web security best practices.&nbsp;

Experience or knowledge of authentication and authorization best practices.&nbsp;

Experience publishing and maintaining&nbsp;npm&nbsp;and&nbsp;NuGet packages.&nbsp;

Knowledge of accessibility best practices.&nbsp;

Knowledge of Rockstar’s game titles and passion&nbsp;for&nbsp;playing video games.&nbsp;
