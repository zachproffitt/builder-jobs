About the Team

The ChatGPT team supports the OpenAI mission by innovating and refining the user experience, technical architecture, and core features of ChatGPT across various platforms. We focus on building scalable, user-centric solutions that empower seamless interactions with ChatGPT, while enabling rapid development and iteration of impactful features that push the boundaries of what is possible through AI.

About the Role

We are looking for a senior software engineer to focus on building and maintaining core features within the ChatGPT android app. In this role, you will:

 - Build and ship new android experiences that expand the boundaries of AI-powered mobile applications, delivering a useful and delightful assistant in every user’s pocket.

 - Drive foundational improvements in the android app, including optimizing performance, establishing robust testing frameworks, refining app architecture, and integrating cutting-edge android platform capabilities.

 - Collaborate on high-impact projects by working closely with product, design, research, and data science teams to achieve product goals and improve user experiences.

 - Prioritize quality and user satisfaction by focusing on performance, reliability, and responsiveness, ensuring a delightful experience for ChatGPT android users.

How We Work

Our team prioritizes excellence through a focus on user delight, intuitive product experiences, and reliability. We take a results-driven approach, concentrating on high-impact projects that maximize value for our users by delivering features that enhance the overall ChatGPT experience. We emphasize product innovation and collaboration to streamline the creation of impactful solutions that meet user needs and exceed expectations.

You Might Thrive in This Role If You:

 - Have a proven track record of developing impactful iOS features, with deep expertise in the Apple ecosystem and related development tools.

 - Enjoy working in cross-functional teams, partnering effectively with product, design, and engineering collaborators.

 - Prioritize performance, reliability, and quality as foundational principles of mobile app development.

 - Are passionate about enabling other developers by designing scalable, maintainable, and high-performing client features.
