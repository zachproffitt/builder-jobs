What You'll Do:

In this role, you'll drive quality at scale by building robust automation frameworks for mobile, web, and game platforms. If you're someone who learns fast, thrives in dynamic environments, and is passionate about automation-first QA, this is your playground.

Design and implement automation frameworks for mobile, web, and game platforms

Automate gameplay features, event-driven flows, and user engagement mechanisms

Systematically convert manual test cases into scalable automated tests

Collaborate with developers and QA to embed automation into CI/CD pipelines

Debug automation failures, analyze test results, and maintain test reliability

Drive continuous improvement in coverage, efficiency, and QA best practices

What We're Looking For:

2-4 years of hands-on experience in test automation

Strong Python skills, especially for async operations and unit testing

Proficiency with Appium or equivalent mobile UI automation tools

Exposure to CI/CD workflows and test integration practices

Basic understanding of Unity or similar game engines (great to have)

Strong problem-solving and debugging skills

A self-starter mindset with a keen eye for quality and efficiency
