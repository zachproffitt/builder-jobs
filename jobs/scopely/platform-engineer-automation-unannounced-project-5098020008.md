Scopely is looking for a Senior Platform Engineer(Automation) to join a new truly unique multiplayer strategy game in Spain, Ireland, Portugal or the UK on a remote or hybrid basis. We can support with visa sponsorship and relocation assistance from any location.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;

At Scopely, we care deeply about what we do and want to inspire play every day - whether in our work environments alongside our talented colleagues or through our deep connections with our communities of players. We are a global team of game lovers who are developing, publishing and innovating the mobile games industry, connecting millions of people around the world daily.&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;

We are in the early stages of development on an ambitious, unannounced Strategy/MMO title, creating a team of talented and passionate game makers to join us on this exciting journey!&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;

What You’ll Do

We are looking for a software engineer with a strong automation mindset to help improve how our engineering teams builds, tests, and deliver softwares.

This role sits at the intersection of automation, reliability, quality, and developer experience. You’ll work on the systems and tooling that enable teams to ship changes safely and repeatedly, reducing manual effort and operational risk through software.

Design, and evolve automation as internal product, to support the software delivery lifecycle, from code commit through to production

Improve the reliability, consistency, and usability of CI/CD pipelines and supporting tooling

Identify manual, error-prone, or slow processes workflows and replace them with automation and a product-driven approach

Collaborate with application engineers, and infrastructure teams to improve delivery safety, resilience, and operational excellence

Contribute to standards and reusable patterns for build, test, deployment, and rollback

Embed reliability, security, and quality checks into automated workflows where they add real value

Improve developer experience through better tooling, documentation, and self-service capabilities

Use metrics and feedback to guide improvements to delivery performance and system reliability

What We’re Looking For

Background as a software engineer, SRE, DevOps engineer, or platform engineer with a strong focus on automation

Ability to quickly navigate and understand unfamiliar codebases, proactively identifying and implementing improvements that enhance software engineering patterns (SOLID, DRY), reliability, observability, and overall system health with minimal supervision

Experience improving developer productivity or internal tooling

Experience operating systems across multiple environments (dev, test, prod)

Strong collaboration and communication skills when working across teams

Experience building or maintaining CI/CD pipelines using tools such as GitHub Actions, GitLab CI, Jenkins, or similar

Comfortable writing automation and tooling in a general-purpose language (e.g. Python, Go, or equivalent)

Working knowledge of cloud environments (AWS, GCP, or Azure), even if not a deep specialist

Familiarity with containerized workloads and modern deployment practices (ECS, Kubernetes/EKS, or similar)

Bonus Points

Exposure to Site Reliability Engineering practices or operational ownership

Experience working with infrastructure as code tools (Terraform, CloudFormation, or similar) is a plus, not a requirement

Familiarity with delivery performance metrics (e.g. deployment frequency, lead time, failure rates)

Interest in software supply chain security or compliance automation

About Scopely

Scopely is a leading video game and global interactive entertainment company, home to many of the world’s most beloved and enduring experiences, including two of the most successful mobile games of all-time “MONOPOLY GO!” and “Pokémon GO,” along with “Stumble Guys,” “Star Trek™ Fleet Command,” “MARVEL Strike Force,” “WWE Champions,” the Scrabble® franchise, “Yahtzee® With Buddies,” and many others. Across mobile, web, PC, and console, Scopely creates, develops, publishes, and live-operates one of the most diversified and award-winning portfolios in the games industry — bringing hundreds of millions of players together through a shared love of play.

Founded in 2011, Scopely is powered by its exceptional team — including thousands of world-class gamemakers around the globe, a distinctive tenet-driven culture, and its proprietary technology platform, Playgami. Together, these strengths have fueled Scopely’s position as the #1 mobile games company in the U.S. and #2 globally, generating more than $10 billion in lifetime revenue. Whether building global sensations like “MONOPOLY GO!” from the ground up, or expanding through strategic acquisitions, including the FoxNext, GSN, and Niantic games businesses — Scopely consistently delivers experiences players love today and return to for years to come.

Recognized multiple times as one of the "100 Most Influential Companies in the World" by TIME magazine and one of Fast Company's "World's Most Innovative Companies" and “Best Workplaces for Innovators,” Scopely believes that video games can be a force for good — creating meaningful connections, vibrant communities, and making life better through play.

Scopely has global operations and partners across four continents in more than a dozen countries worldwide. For more information, visit: https://www.scopely.com/.&nbsp;
