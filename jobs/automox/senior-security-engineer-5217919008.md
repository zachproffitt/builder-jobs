We’re looking for a proactive and organized Senior Security Engineer to support the day-to-day rhythm of our Security team. WHAT YOU'LL OWN &amp; DRIVE

Build proactive security automation aimed at decreasing manual remediation work.

Research new and novel ways to accomplish security work and publish your findings on our blog.

Participate in a monthly security on-call rotation for critical escalations.&nbsp;

Build security capabilities utilizing the attacker mindset and other adversary research.

&nbsp;

WHAT YOU BRING TO THE TABLE

You have 4+ years of experience in a security domain and exposure to SaaS companies.

You have experience automating tasks in a programming language of your choice (Python, Go, Node.js, etc.).

You can triage security issues appropriately; accounting for reachability and business impact.

You have experience participating in a security program at a mid-stage startup where every decision involves balancing competing priorities.

You enjoy having philosophical discussions about security practices and ways to improve them.

You want a chance to try something new without much bureaucratic overhead.
