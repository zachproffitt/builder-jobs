Position Overview:

As a Senior Technical Artist, you will serve as a versatile problem-solver, bridging the gap between art and technology. Your primary responsibility will be to craft tools, shaders, animations and code-driven behaviors, setting the stage for our Art team to deliver their final polish and taking care of every visual problem that arises in the game. You’ll thrive in a role that demands quick adaptation to new workflows, investigation of innovative solutions, and the ability to diagnose and resolve pipeline challenges with precision and logic.

What You’ll Do:

Design and implement functional UI and 3d tooling that align with our high visual standards deliverables.

Senior Technical Artist’s primary duties will be integrating art assets (prefabs 2d and 3d), animations, VFX in the game, using the Unity game engine

Investigate and resolve implementation challenges stemming from UX wireframes and animatics.

Collaborate with art, design, and development teams to meet to solve technical issues

Debug and resolve technical issues across a broad range of supported devices.

Leverage coding skills to create code-hooks for triggering UI animations as needed.

Profile and optimize visual effects and art assets for performance and efficiency.

Develop and maintain relevant art team documentation.

Evaluate emerging software and art-authoring techniques to enhance workflows.

Provide front-line support for pipeline issues and feature requests from the UI art team.

What You Bring:

6+ years of industry experience with Unity engine game development.

Proficiency in C# programming, with knowledge of at least one additional programming language.

Ability to collaborate effectively with Design, UI/UX, Development, and QA teams to ensure smooth project execution.

Clear understanding of cameras, lighting, and shaders in Unity.

Proficiency of Unity Timeline and experience with Unity’s VFX system (Shuriken).

Strong grasp of optimization techniques, with experience using profiling tools and managing art content for frame rate and memory budgets.

Experience optimizing for mobile devices and familiarity with Xcode/Android compilers (desirable but not required).

Proven ability to author content in After Effects and experience with HLSL/Cg shader coding (desirable but not required).

Familiarity with Git, Perforce, or similar version control software.

A passion for games and social networking.
