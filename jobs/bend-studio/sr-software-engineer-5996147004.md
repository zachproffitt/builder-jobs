Responsibilities:

Drive video codecs architecture design and development for PlayStation SDKs and cloud gaming platform.

Own multiple encoder/decoder features with strong accountability, leveraging ML-based techniques for video quality enhancement

Partner with platform, hardware, and system software teams to investigate and resolve complex, high-priority issues across the codec pipeline.

Evaluate emerging codec and media technologies and recommend integration strategies for future platforms.
