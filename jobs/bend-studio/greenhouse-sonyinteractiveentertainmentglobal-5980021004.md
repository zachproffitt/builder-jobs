Sony Interactive Entertainment isn’t just the Best Place to Play — it’s also the Best Place to Work. Sony Interactive Entertainment (SIE) is the company behind the PlayStation brand. Our role at SIE is to create and nurture the experiences under the PlayStation brand, a name synonymous with entertainment excellence and creativity.

As a part of Sony Interactive Entertainment, GDFT is leading the cloud gaming revolution, putting console-quality video games on any device, from TVs to consoles to mobile devices and beyond.

The Hardware Fleet Operations team is a critical component of our support structure that enables GDFT to successfully operate a global footprint at scale. This role focuses on Linux terminal-based troubleshooting beyond documented workflows, investigating recurring issues, managing the hardware lifecycle, and improving documentation and runbooks. You will partner with various engineering groups to transition operational support work to the Hardware Fleet Operations team.&nbsp;

The role also includes building lightweight scripts or basic automation to reduce manual effort and enable other support teams to handle repeatable tasks more efficiently.

&nbsp;

This role is hybrid and based in Aliso Viejo, CA.

Contract / Contingent Worker status, 12-month contract

&nbsp;

Key Responsibilities

Respond to operational support requests in a 24/5 environment, meeting SLAs and prioritizing work across internal customers and cross-functional teams.

Troubleshoot and support Linux-based hardware systems using terminal-based tools, addressing issues that require investigation beyond existing documentation.

Identify recurring operational issues and work with engineering teams to stabilize and operationalize support workflows.

Create, update, and maintain runbooks, troubleshooting guides, and internal documentation based on real-world support findings.

Develop and maintain lightweight scripts or simple automation to reduce manual tasks and improve operational consistency.

Support the transition of operational workloads from engineering teams into the Hardware Fleet Operations team.

&nbsp;

Who We Are Looking For

2-3 years of hands-on experience supporting or troubleshooting Linux-based systems.

Prior experience in a technical support, operations, or IT service delivery role.

Comfortable in working primarily in Linux terminal environments.

Experience following and improving runbooks, documentation, and operational processes.

Familiarity with ticketing systems such as Jira or ServiceNow in an operations context.

Strong written and verbal communication skills, with the ability to clearly document issues and solutions for technical and non-technical audiences.

&nbsp;

Preferred Qualifications

Linux terminal-based troubleshooting

Basic scripting or automation (e.g., Bash, Python, or similar)

IT support fundamentals, including systems and basic networking concepts (Network+ level or equivalent)
