Job Title – QA Automation Engineer &nbsp;
Location –&nbsp;Ireland, Dublin&nbsp;
This role is responsible for ensuring the quality, performance, and reliability of Adobe Experience Manager (AEM) applications through a combination of manual and automated testing. You will work closely with cross-functional teams in an agile environment to validate new features and support scalable, secure product development. The position plays a key role in delivering high-quality releases by identifying defects, improving test coverage, and contributing to continuous integration practices.
What you'll be doing:&nbsp;
Design, create and execute manual and automated test plans and scripts to test the functionality and performance of AEM (Adobe Experience Manager) sites
Solving complex business problems while ensuring the product is highly scalable, secure, and performant
In depth, functional, regression, and performance testing of new or enhanced features in the application.
Work in an agile and highly collaborative environment with our globally distributed engineering teams, architecture, product management, quality assurance, and operations.
Keen on delivering high quality and low to zero defect releases
Responsible to analyze, report, and monitor software defects and performs re-testing
Share knowledge with the team through training, defect documentation and by facilitating meetings
Exposure on various Test Automation Frameworks.&nbsp;
Develop and execute Automated testing scripts using Java, Selenium WebDriver, Applitools, Cucumber, etc.
Experience in setting up Jenkins and configuring it with Maven to aid Continuous Integration/Development&nbsp;
What we're looking for:
BSc/MSc or equivalent in Computer Science or related field. You must show a combination of technical skills, and a strong product development background.
3+ years of experience as a Quality Assurance Engineer
3+ years of experience in designing, developing, and executing test plans and test scripts
3+ years of experience in Adobe Experience Manager and related Adobe products.
At least 2 years of test automation experience on Java and Selenium
Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
Experienced in testing and supporting Web and Mobile applications
Responsible for creating an end-to-end test plan and executing the test cases.
Technical ability to triage issues and defects in the project JIRA board.
Having experience in Web Applications (REST APIs), Postman, knowledge of XML, X-path.&nbsp;
Preferred Qualities&nbsp;
Understanding of the Gaming industry is a plus
Knowledge of AEM, CRXDE or another comparable CMS tool
Proficiency in Test Case and Defect Management tools, such as JIRA, Wrike, QTest and Github
Organized self-starter with drive and commitment
Demonstrable ability to prioritize work and manage multiple demands
Passionate about gaming and video game culture&nbsp;
