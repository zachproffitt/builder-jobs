Why Sony Interactive Entertainment?

Sony Interactive Entertainment isn’t just the Best Place to Play — it’s also the Best Place to Work. Sony Interactive Entertainment (SIE) is the company behind the PlayStation brand. As a subsidiary of Sony Group Corporation, we’re part of a proud legacy of innovation and excellence. SIE is a dynamic technology company, delivering cutting-edge hardware and network services to more than 100 million people and an entertainment leader, home to some of the most beloved and recognizable intellectual properties (IP) in the world. Our role at SIE is to create and nurture the experiences under the PlayStation brand, a name synonymous with entertainment excellence and creativity.

Position Overview: &nbsp;

How would you like to work on a global team developing industry leading audio software for the PlayStation platforms? Do you want to craft the future of game audio? Are you the type of person who gets excited when discussing audio algorithms and optimizations? If this sounds like you, we have a unique opportunity to join Sony PlayStation on our Platform Audio team! &nbsp;

Our Platform Audio team seeks an enthusiastic software engineer who can build and implement technology used for 3D audio&nbsp;and&nbsp;hardware DSP&nbsp;acceleration.&nbsp;Your contributions&nbsp;will&nbsp;directly&nbsp;affect both our console users and game developers.&nbsp;If this is you, please apply! &nbsp;

Responsibilities: &nbsp;

Systems&nbsp;level multi-threaded programming&nbsp;and debugging using C/C++ &nbsp;

Implement&nbsp;DSP algorithms on embedded&nbsp;audio hardware accelerators &nbsp;

Design and implement&nbsp;new features of the audio API for our platform SDK&nbsp;

Collaborate with international counterparts to contribute to the&nbsp;design,&nbsp;development,&nbsp;and evaluation&nbsp;of audio&nbsp;algorithms&nbsp;and&nbsp;APIs&nbsp;

Qualifications: &nbsp;

Four years or more of embedded systems programming using&nbsp;C/C++ &nbsp;

Experience&nbsp;as&nbsp;a&nbsp;primary&nbsp;contributor&nbsp;in&nbsp;a&nbsp;similar&nbsp;field (audio, acoustics,&nbsp;DSP)&nbsp;

Experience with&nbsp;low-level system architecture&nbsp;concepts&nbsp;such&nbsp;as memory mapping, DMA,&nbsp;multi-threading, assembly code&nbsp;

Proficient with&nbsp;standard software development tools, source control, and issue tracking&nbsp;

Experience with&nbsp;SIMD&nbsp;vectorization&nbsp;and optimizations for real-time performance&nbsp;

Experience profiling&nbsp;and debugging performance bottlenecks in audio systems&nbsp;

Bachelor's degree or equivalent

Nice to have: &nbsp;

Competency&nbsp;in digital signal processing (sampling,&nbsp;filtering,&nbsp;FFT, convolution)&nbsp;

Prior exposure&nbsp;to game audio&nbsp;/&nbsp;audio&nbsp;middleware&nbsp;

Experience with&nbsp;GPU development&nbsp;

Knowledge in&nbsp;psychoacoustics and 3D audio&nbsp;(HRTFs, object-based audio)&nbsp;
