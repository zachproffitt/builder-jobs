Why Sony Interactive Entertainment?
Sony Interactive Entertainment isn’t just the Best Place to Play — it’s also the Best Place to Work. Sony Interactive Entertainment (SIE) is the company behind the PlayStation brand. As a subsidiary of Sony Group Corporation, we’re part of a proud legacy of innovation and excellence. SIE is a dynamic technology company, delivering cutting-edge hardware and network services to more than 100 million people and an entertainment leader, home to some of the most beloved and recognizable intellectual properties (IP) in the world. Our role at SIE is to create and nurture the experiences under the PlayStation brand, a name synonymous with entertainment excellence and creativity.
You will be a key member of our backend team and determine the core infrastructure of our next generation platforms and services to enhance our PlayStation ecosystem.&nbsp;
&nbsp;
Responsibilities:&nbsp;
Contribute to the development of backend systems that power the PlayStation platform as part of a collaborative Scrum team.
Work closely with other developers and teams to support the design and implementation of platform services.
Build and maintain scalable services using Node.js and TypeScript/JavaScript.
Write clean, maintainable, and well-tested code, including unit tests for features and bug fixes.
Assist in troubleshooting, debugging, and resolving technical issues.
Participate in code reviews and contribute to improving overall code quality.
Create and maintain technical documentation for your work.
Continuously learn and grow by engaging with senior engineers and adopting best practices in software development.
&nbsp;
Required Qualifications:&nbsp;
Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related field, or equivalent.
3+ years of experience in backend or full-stack development.
Direct experience with Node.js, JavaScript, and/or TypeScript.
Knowledge of RESTful APIs and general web service principles.
Familiarity Exposure to cloud technologies such as AWS, Azure, or GCP.
Strong problem-solving and communication skills, with an eagerness to learn and take feedback.
Comfortable working in a collaborative, agile team environment.
Experience working with GraphQL or RESTful APIs.
Knowledge of version control systems such as Git and familiarity with pull request workflows.
Interest in CI/CD pipelines and understanding the basics of automated builds or testing.
Awareness of monitoring tools or performance metrics for backend services.
Familiarity with frontend frameworks or Server-Side Rendering (SSR) tools such as Next.js is a plus.
Coursework or exposure to software testing, data structures, or distributed systems concepts.
Familiarity with other Server Side Languages
