Why Sony Interactive Entertainment?
Sony Interactive Entertainment isn’t just the Best Place to Play — it’s also the Best Place to Work. Sony Interactive Entertainment (SIE) is the company behind the PlayStation brand. As a subsidiary of Sony Group Corporation, we’re part of a proud legacy of innovation and excellence. SIE is a dynamic technology company, delivering cutting-edge hardware and network services to more than 100 million people and an entertainment leader, home to some of the most beloved and recognizable intellectual properties (IP) in the world. Our role at SIE is to create and nurture the experiences under the PlayStation brand, a name synonymous with entertainment excellence and creativity.
About the Role
As a Software Engineer II at PlayStation, you will be a key contributor to a team focused on providing highly impactful UI experiences to our users! This role fits someone keen to learn, grow, and support our evolving technology stack. You will develop insight into our user base and collaborate with multiple teams to ensure we’re delivering the right outcome. The ideal candidate will be adaptable, flexible in approach, and ready to face ambitious challenges.&nbsp;
About you
Passionate about UI development and enjoy working with consumer facing products
Collaborated across different team roles including product managers and designers to parse out key requirements and translate them to technical solutions
Passionate about quality and strives to improve processes, proposes standard methodologies and drives their usage within the teams
Debugging and tackling technical issues in not only your code but your fellow developer’s code as well
Have a test driven development mentality with goals to prevent regression of code
Work independently in a fast-paced ever-evolving environment
Work with other team members by reviewing and giving feedback on pull requests and design plans
Collaborate well with engineers internal and external to your teams to test ideas and optimize solutions
Continually seeking to improve your knowledge and skills, and using your knowledge to help elevate your peers
Required Qualifications
Bachelor’s degree or equivalent
2+ years of building product experiences with proficiencies in UI technologies (JavaScript, React, Typescript, node..etc)
Experience constructing robust unit tests and familiarity with other types of software testing
Proficiency in profiling performance and optimizing code in your domain, with knowledge to navigate conversations across the tech stack.
Excellent communication skills, and the ability to help non technical&nbsp; team members (product, design) navigate technical implementation challenges
Bonus Qualifications
Experience with React Native and multi application environments
Experience with Jest, React Testing Library
Experience building integration or component testing.
Understanding of build pipelines and CI/CD standards
Experience with Agile/Scrum software development methodologies
This role is located in San Mateo, CA. When you're not building the best gaming experience possible, you'll have access to extraordinary food, wine country, hiking, and all that Silicon Valley has to offer.
We have a very flexible work-from-home policy as well: live close enough to the office for occasional in-person meetings and events, otherwise, work in the office as much or as little as you like!
