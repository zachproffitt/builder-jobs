Role Overview:
The People Technology Integration Engineer, Workday&nbsp;is responsible for designing, building, and evolving integration and data solutions across SIE’s global Workday platform and broader HR technology ecosystem.
This role operates as an independent contributor within People Technology Services, owning the design and delivery of scalable, secure, and reliable system integrations. The role focuses on enabling seamless data movement, system connectivity, and interoperability across platforms, ensuring that integrations are maintainable, performant, and aligned with architectural standards.
Working within an Agile product delivery model, this role contributes to both the continuous evolution of integration capabilities and the stability of core data flows. The Integration Engineer partners closely with Platform Engineers, Product Managers, and Data teams to deliver cohesive, end-to-end technology solutions.
This role is suited for individuals with strong technical depth who are motivated to build and improve integration architecture and data pipelines within an enterprise environment.
What you'll be doing:
Integration Engineering &amp; Solution Design
Design, build, and implement scalable integration solutions across the Workday platform and connected systems, including:
Workday Studio
Core Connectors and EIBs
API-based integrations (REST/SOAP)
Define and apply integration design patterns that promote scalability, reusability, and maintainability
Design secure and reliable data flows, including transformation logic, orchestration, and error handling
Evaluate integration approaches and make informed design decisions, balancing business needs, system constraints, and long-term sustainability
Own end-to-end delivery of integration solutions, including design, build, testing, and deployment readiness
Integration Architecture &amp; Platform Connectivity
Contribute to the design and evolution of integration architecture across the People Technology ecosystem
Ensure consistent patterns for system connectivity, data exchange, and interface design
Document integration designs, data flows, and technical architecture to support maintainability and governance
Collaborate with multi-functional teams to enable seamless end-to-end system interactions
Data Engineering &amp; Reporting Solutions
Design and deliver data extracts, reporting solutions, and document generation capabilities (e.g., BIRT, Docs for Layouts) as part of the broader People data ecosystem
Develop and maintain calculated fields and data structures to support integration and reporting use cases
Ensure data accuracy, integrity, and consistency across integrations and downstream systems
Platform Reliability &amp; Operations
Maintain reliability of integration pipelines through proactive monitoring, issue diagnosis, and resolution
Identify root causes of failures and implement structural improvements to enhance stability and performance
Optimize integrations for performance, scalability, and fault tolerance
Partner with team members to prioritize enhancements and manage competing demands
Delivery &amp; Agile Execution
Translate business and technical requirements into well-defined integration solutions
Participate actively in Agile delivery, including sprint planning, backlog refinement, and retrospectives
Own and deliver assigned workstreams within sprint cycles
Execute and support testing activities (unit, integration, UAT), ensuring end-to-end data validation
Collaborate with Platform Engineers and functional partners to deliver integrated, cross-domain solutions
Governance, Security &amp; Quality
Ensure all integration solutions meet data privacy, security, and audit requirements
Implement secure data exchange practices, including encryption, authentication, and secure file transfer (e.g., SFTP)
Follow established governance, release management, and change control practices
Ensure integration solutions align with platform architecture and engineering standards
Contribute to improving integration standards, documentation, and engineering practices
What we're looking for:
5+ years of hands-on experience with enterprise platforms, including design and delivery of Workday integrations
Demonstrated experience building and deploying integrations using:
Workday Studio
Core Connectors and EIBs
API-based integrations (REST/SOAP)
Proven track record to design and troubleshoot integration solutions, including data transformation, interface logic, and failure handling
Experience implementing secure data exchange mechanisms, including encryption and secure file transfer
Hands-on experience developing Workday reports (Advanced, Matrix, Composite) and calculated fields
Solid understanding of the Workday Core HCM data model and data flows across integrations and business processes
Experience translating requirements into scalable technical solutions, including evaluating trade-offs between speed, complexity, and maintainability
Experience working in Agile delivery environments, with ownership of defined workstreams or sub-projects
Ability to clearly articulate personal contributions to delivered solutions, including specific integrations and technical components

Experience working within global enterprise environments across multiple regions
Experience contributing to integration standards, architecture, or engineering practices
Profile
