Why Sony Interactive Entertainment?

Sony Interactive Entertainment isn’t just the Best Place to Play — it’s also the Best Place to Work. Sony Interactive Entertainment (SIE) is the company behind the PlayStation brand. As a subsidiary of Sony Group Corporation, we’re part of a proud legacy of innovation and excellence. SIE is a dynamic technology company, delivering cutting-edge hardware and network services to more than 100 million people and an entertainment leader, home to some of the most beloved and recognizable intellectual properties (IP) in the world. Our role at SIE is to create and nurture the experiences under the PlayStation brand, a name synonymous with entertainment excellence and creativity.

We have an exciting opportunity at Sony Interactive Entertainment LLC (PlayStation). Our software production organization seeks a software engineer for our Platform Audio team. This team is a trailblazer in audio technology. Efforts here will contribute to the development of system libraries used for both our current and next generation PlayStation products. You will drive the design and implementation of technology used for 3D audio, hardware DSP, and virtual reality. The software you develop here is the foundation of an SDK for industry leading game and content developers and will change the way audio is used worldwide. If this is you, please apply!&nbsp;

Responsibilities:

Drive algorithm development and implementation in spatial audio, psychoacoustics, and other audio related fields including HRTF (capture, synthesis, personalization), Ambisonics, loudspeaker rendering, and more

Develop requirements based on research or user input and assist with platform integration

Systems level multi-threaded programming and debugging using C and C++

Collaborate with international counterparts to contribute to PlayStation’s Tempest 3D Audio technology and our greater audio ecosystem

Qualifications:

5 years or more of digital signal processing experience

Proficient in: C/C++, Python

Experience as a technical contributor to a project involving spatial audio

Solid understanding of basic acoustic and psychoacoustic principles

Bachelor's degree or equivalent

Nice-to-Have:

Basic source control experience (git)

Knowledge of audio production tools and software (e.g. Reaper, VST Development)

Embedded systems development

Graphics or GPU programming experience

ML/AI modeling and training experience

Experience in developing novel audio technologies
