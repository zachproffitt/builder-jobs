Why Sony Interactive Entertainment?

Sony Interactive Entertainment isn’t just the Best Place to Play — it’s also the Best Place to Work. Sony Interactive Entertainment (SIE) is the company behind the PlayStation brand. As a subsidiary of Sony Group Corporation, we’re part of a proud legacy of innovation and excellence. SIE is a dynamic technology company, delivering cutting-edge hardware and network services to more than 100 million people and an entertainment leader, home to some of the most beloved and recognizable intellectual properties (IP) in the world. Our role at SIE is to create and nurture the experiences under the PlayStation brand, a name synonymous with entertainment excellence and creativity.

Job Title – Software Engineer (AEM)&nbsp;

Location –&nbsp;Ireland, Dublin&nbsp;

&nbsp;

This is a phenomenal opportunity for&nbsp;a &nbsp;Software&nbsp;Engineer&nbsp;(AEM) to help build, lead, and scale SIE’s&nbsp;Martech&nbsp;Engineering team, and&nbsp;be responsible for&nbsp;the development of PlayStation.com and delivery of internal and consumer facing web applications. Our ideal candidate will be a highly effective and motivated AEM developer who can&nbsp;participate&nbsp;and contribute to the entire software development lifecycle including analysis, technical design, implementation,&nbsp;testing&nbsp;and deployment of the applications. They will work closely with architects, developers, quality engineers, DevOps&nbsp;to support and enhance our digital platform. The role will deliver assigned tasks/solutions on time and escalate issues when&nbsp;appropriate, including educating and leading others in the form of code reviews, workshops, and documentation.&nbsp;

&nbsp;

What you'll be doing:

The AEM developer will play a vital role in the development, design, testing and deployment of AEM Assets and Sites, as well as Adobe Marketing Cloud tools.

Share new concepts, strategies or innovative approaches to development execution that will have significant impact on the project.

Provide deep knowledge expertise in AEM and working knowledge of Adobe Target and Analytics.

Ensure we are developing using web best practices including areas such as user experience, SEO, A/B testing, and website performance optimization.

Employ strong coding standards for efficiency, readability, and reuse of code.

Troubleshoot issues by reviewing or debugging code, provide fixes and workarounds, suggest changes for operability and maintaining existing project solutions.

Provides technical guidance and mentorship to peers.

Ensures software developed adheres to best practices and quality standards through code and design reviews.

Apply an AI-first experimentation mentality to rapidly iterate on technical proof-of-concepts, assessing and validating business value outcomes.

Collaborate with Product Owners to ensure technical solutions align with business requirements and defined processes.&nbsp;

&nbsp;

What we're looking for:

BS or MS degree in Computer Science/Information Technology or equivalent experience with Software Engineering.

You must show a combination of technical skills, leadership skills, and a strong product development background.

6-8 years of recent and progressive experience in AEM Content Management, AEM developer tools.

Strong experience with AEM building blocks including templates, components, dialogs, widgets, and bundles.

Use AI-powered technologies to optimize code quality and development lifecycles&nbsp;

Experience and in-depth knowledge of Experience fragments, Content fragments and SPA.

Experience migrating AEM Sites and Assets to AEM as a Cloud Service AEMaaCS including planning and execution.

Ability to refactor and make legacy AMS code cloud compatible addressing deprecated features and Cloud Manager quality gates.

Familiarity with Java concepts and patterns, including Java/J2EE, JSP, Sling, web services, JUnit, MVC, Eclipse, and Maven.

Expertise with Adobe Experience Cloud implementations and enterprise customer use cases, with specific experience with AEM as a Cloud Service, including Edge Delivery Services.&nbsp;

Experience in JCR suite, Sling web framework and Apache Felix OSGI framework.

Experience with project automation technologies like: Jenkins, Automated unit testing, Maven, SonarQube, Jira and GitHub.

Working knowledge of web technologies like Tomcat, Apache http, Content delivery networks (CDN, Akamai).

Experience working with APIs, integrations, JSON structures, and system-to-system data flows.

Proficient in front-end technologies, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and ReactJS.

Knowledge of Search engines, CDN, middleware integrations, Load Balancers.

Identify and resolve performance and scalability issues.

Working in Agile development practices and scrum model.

Strong knowledge of technical documentation processes/procedures.

Flexibility to work with geographically diverse team and familiar with the onsite/offshore working models.&nbsp;

&nbsp;

Preferred Qualities&nbsp;

Experience executing projects with Jira and/or Asana is a plus.

AEM Cloud Migration expertise.

Understanding of Algolia Search.

Adobe Certified Expert as an AEM Developer or AEM Architect is preferred.

Web application development experience, including React, NextJS, NodeJS.

Passionate about gaming and video game culture.
