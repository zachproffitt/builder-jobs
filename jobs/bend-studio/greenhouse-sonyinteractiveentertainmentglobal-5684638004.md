Responsibilities&nbsp;
Implement and optimize peer-to-peer PCIe communication between DPUs and other bus devices, minimizing latency and host CPU involvement&nbsp;
Manage network device queues across PCIe peers and handle doorbell interrupts to process them.&nbsp;
Architect innovative solutions and drive alignment across teams and stakeholders.&nbsp;
Collaborate across hardware, drivers, system software and network cross functional teams.&nbsp;
Use hardware and software debugging tools to diagnose PCIe and DPU issues.&nbsp;
Document key technical areas and share knowledge through technical presentations &nbsp;
&nbsp;&nbsp;
Required Qualifications&nbsp;
BS in Computer Science / Software Engineering or 10+ years of relevant work experience&nbsp;
8+ years of combined experience in C/C++&nbsp;
Expert level proficiency with DPDK and Software Defined Networking
Strong understanding of PCIe peer-to-peer communication, including memory-mapped I/O, queue management, and device-to-device signaling.&nbsp;
Ability to debug, profile, and optimize firmware performance&nbsp;
Working knowledge of networking protocols: TCP/IP, UDP and L2 layer protocols&nbsp;
Proven track-record of solving ambiguous problems with minimal direction.&nbsp;
Strong debugging / analysis / troubleshooting skills&nbsp;
&nbsp;
&nbsp;Preferred Qualifications&nbsp;
Experience with developing software/firmware on&nbsp;DPUs, or similar technologies
Experience with Open vSwitch/Sonic&nbsp;
EVPN-VXLAN knowledge&nbsp;
Network driver development experience on Linux or embedded OS or similar&nbsp;
Experience with storage offload engines and computer networking domains &nbsp;
A track record of technical leadership guiding teams without hard authority.&nbsp;
&nbsp;
Additional Skills&nbsp;
Motivated self-starter.&nbsp;
Experience with Rust.&nbsp;
