Why Sony Interactive Entertainment?
Sony Interactive Entertainment isn’t just the Best Place to Play — it’s also the Best Place to Work. Sony Interactive Entertainment (SIE) is the company behind the PlayStation brand. As a subsidiary of Sony Group Corporation, we’re part of a proud legacy of innovation and excellence. SIE is a dynamic technology company, delivering cutting-edge hardware and network services to more than 100 million people and an entertainment leader, home to some of the most beloved and recognizable intellectual properties (IP) in the world. Our role at SIE is to create and nurture the experiences under the PlayStation brand, a name synonymous with entertainment excellence and creativity.
Job Summary
Join PlayStation as a Tools &amp; Build Software Engineer to drive the future of CI/CD, designing and optimizing scalable pipelines for global game development. This role offers the chance to lead critical initiatives, mentor talent, and shape best practices in a dynamic entertainment leader.
Job Role Responsibilities:
Design, develop, and maintain scalable CI/CD pipelines across diverse platforms (PlayStation consoles, Web, Mobile, PC) with a focus on Jenkins and GitHub Actions
Modernize and standardize build, test, and release workflows across teams, ensuring reliability, speed, and developer efficiency
Partner closely with engineering, QA, and operations teams to understand their workflows and proactively build solutions that remove friction
Own the strategy for pipeline health: implement monitoring, automated recovery, and continuous improvements to minimize build/test failures and downtime
Develop tools and frameworks to improve build reproducibility, dependency management, and artifact handling
Collaborate with global teams to drive alignment on CI/CD strategy and support large-scale software delivery at PlayStation
Minimum Requirements:
Strong understanding of software engineering fundamentals, including object-oriented principles, data structures, and algorithms
Experience with version control (Git, GitHub)
Hands-on experience with Jenkins and/or GitHub Actions at scale
Experience with containerization (Docker) and working with Linux-based build environments
Proven ability to design and maintain scalable build and automation pipelines in complex engineering environments
Excellent problem-solving and debugging skills in CI/CD contexts
Preferred Requirements:
Experience leading CI/CD initiatives or driving adoption of new build/release systems across multiple teams
Experience with Kubernetes or similar orchestration technologies
Knowledge of Linux and Windows build environments, including cross-compilation or multi-platform support
Familiarity with build acceleration techniques (caching, parallelization, artifact repositories)
Excellent communication and collaboration skills, with the ability to influence across engineering organizations
