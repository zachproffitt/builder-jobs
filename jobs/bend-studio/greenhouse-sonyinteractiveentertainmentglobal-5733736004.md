Position Overview:

We have an exciting opportunity at Sony Interactive Entertainment LLC (PlayStation). Our software production organization seeks a senior-level software engineer for our&nbsp;Video Codecs&nbsp;team. This team is a trailblazer in&nbsp;video encoding/decoding&nbsp;technology. Efforts here will contribute to the development of system libraries used for both our current and next&nbsp;generation&nbsp;PlayStation products. You will contribute to the design and implementation of technology used for&nbsp;cloud gaming,&nbsp;remote play,&nbsp;game recording,&nbsp;video&nbsp;streaming&nbsp;and&nbsp;in-game video playback. The software you develop here is the foundation of an SDK for industry leading&nbsp;game&nbsp;and content developers and will change the way&nbsp;video&nbsp;games&nbsp;are&nbsp;played&nbsp;worldwide. If this is you, please apply!&nbsp;

Responsibilities:

Drive video codecs architecture design and development for PlayStation SDKs and cloud gaming platform.

Own multiple encoder/decoder features with strong accountability for quality, performance, and maintainability.

Conduct system-level performance investigations and lead optimization strategies across CPU and GPU.

Partner with platform, hardware, and system software teams to investigate and resolve complex, high-priority issues across the codec pipeline.

Evaluate emerging codec and media technologies and recommend integration strategies for future platforms.

Required Qualifications:

Extensive experience with GPU programming (HLSL, Vulkan, CUDA) with proven skills in profiling, debugging, and optimizations across CPU and GPU.

Familiarity with video compression standards (e.g. H.264/H.265, VP9).

Knowledge of multi-threaded programming and debugging using C and C++

Proficient with a wide range of development tools, including IDEs, Git/SVN, JIRA

Proven ability to deliver features from design to validation with clean, efficient code.

Bachelor's degree or equivalent

Nice to have:

ML/AI modeling and training experience

Embedded system experience with multimedia frameworks

Knowledge of SIMD&nbsp;optimizations on real time systems

Contributions to open-source projects in multimedia, systems programming, or performance engineering.
