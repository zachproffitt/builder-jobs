Senior Analytics Engineer&nbsp;

The Opportunity:&nbsp;

The Senior Analytics Engineer will be responsible for empowering analysts and data-driven stakeholders by owning the architecture and infrastructure by which we ingest, transform, test, document, and connect data across Grafana Labs. Critical to the success of this role will be consistent communication with end stakeholders (analysts and business partners) in order to build a data stack that is flexible enough to meet the needs of our business as we grow.

What You’ll Be Doing:

Own, operate, and maintain the architecture and infrastructure of our data stack (e.g. BigQuery, dbt, Fivetran)

Ensure that the data we need to understand and serve our stakeholders is available, accurate, and accessible

Partner with GTM, Self-Serve, Marketing, Finance, and BizApps (and other stakeholders) to understand their data needs and identify places where the analytics stack can improve on what they’re doing

Become the go-to expert on our data pipeline, data tools, and what our data means

Create data processes and standard operating procedures that leverage AI agent skills and rules

Create and own best practices for version control, documentation, testing, and other data-related areas

What Makes You a Great Fit:

3-5 years experience contributing to data modeling

Experience with dbt

Experience working with a wide variety of peers and stakeholders, from business users to analysts to data engineers

Experience creating and maintaining data quality tests and logic tests (e.g. unit tests)

Established track record enabling stakeholders to use data by various means such as documentation, training, and cataloging

A passion for understanding business questions and making data driven insights. Excellent analytical skills

An understanding of coding agents &amp; agentic coding practices and patterns

Experience working in cloud environments such as AWS or GCP is a plus!

Familiarity with the monitoring, observability, log aggregation, or APM market is a plus!

Strong analytical, data processing, and problem-solving skills

Exceptional attention to detail

Strong verbal and written communications skills with the ability to effectively communicate with all levels of employees and provide support globally

Experience implementing best practices for analytics, dashboarding, documentation, and testing

Experience building collaboration and rapport within an organization with both technical and non-technical members

Experience working in a “remote first” environment

Empathetic, understanding, and humble

Strong work ethic

Experience working well in both a team environment and independently

Inquisitive demeanor with willingness to learn new technologies and responsibilities
