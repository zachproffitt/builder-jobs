Grafana Labs is a remote-first, open-source powerhouse. There are more than 20M users of Grafana, the open source visualization tool, around the globe, monitoring everything from beehives to climate change in the Alps. The instantly recognizable dashboards have been spotted everywhere from a NASA launch and Minecraft HQ to Wimbledon and the Tour de France. Grafana Labs also helps more than 3,000 companies -- including Bloomberg, JPMorgan Chase, and eBay -- manage their observability strategies with the Grafana LGTM Stack, which can be run fully managed with Grafana Cloud or self-managed with the Grafana Enterprise Stack, both featuring scalable metrics (Grafana Mimir), logs (Grafana Loki), and traces (Grafana Tempo).

We’re scaling fast and staying true to what makes us different: an open-source legacy, a global collaborative culture, and a passion for meaningful work. Our team thrives in an innovation-driven environment where transparency, autonomy, and trust fuel everything we do.

You may not meet every requirement, and that’s okay. If this role excites you, we’d love you to raise your hand for what could be a truly career-defining opportunity.

This is a remote opportunity and we would be interested in applicants based in Spain, Sweden, Germany, the UK or Ireland at this time.&nbsp;

The Opportunity:&nbsp;

&nbsp;

The Drilldown team builds intuitive, guided workflows that make complex telemetry easy to explore and understand.&nbsp; We let users navigate seamlessly through their signals, facilitating their ability to gain insights and identify issues. Our mission is to design and maintain intuitive, opinionated, queryless user experiences that empower users to quickly answer telemetry questions through simple point-and-click interactions.

What we do:

Guided Exploration: We build simple, intuitive UIs that help users troubleshoot and answer questions without writing queries.

Cross-Data Insights: We seek opportunities to integrate the Drilldown plugins with other parts of the Grafana ecosystem, in order to provide a seamless experience to users.

Community Driven: We listen to users and contributors, and turn feedback into real improvements.

Operational Excellence: We write robust, extensible code and own qualification and delivery of our plugin.&nbsp; We monitor usage and performance and always look for ways to improve our functionality and increase the efficiency of our workflows.

How we work:

No blame: We cultivate a growth mindset, with a focus on learning and improving ourselves and our product.

Better done than perfect: We iterate quickly and deliver value continuously.

Experimentation: We develop ideas for new offerings and try them out, then monitor their usage and user feedback on them.

Performance is non-negotiable: Drilldown must always feel responsive and interactive.

Mentorship &amp; Innovation: we grow together through knowledge sharing, mentorship, internal hackathons, and constant learning.

&nbsp;

Support and mentor team members, engage in design conversations, and work closely with colleagues.
