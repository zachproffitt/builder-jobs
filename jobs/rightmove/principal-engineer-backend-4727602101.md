The Role…&nbsp;

Rightmove's consumer product-domain&nbsp;builds&nbsp;what most people picture when they think of Rightmove. Property search, listing pages, saved searches, maps, alerts, valuations. It gets an enormous amount of traffic,&nbsp;it's&nbsp;used at one of the most stressful moments in people's lives, and around 60 engineers work across it every day.&nbsp;

We're&nbsp;hiring a Principal Engineer into this domain.&nbsp;This is a senior, cross-domain role for someone who wants to drive impact across multiple teams, deepen our architectural thinking, and help us raise the bar on technical execution, product delivery, and long-term platform health.&nbsp;

Your technical grounding is in Java and Spring Boot, and&nbsp;you're&nbsp;genuinely expert there. But you think and&nbsp;operate&nbsp;well beyond your specialism: across front-end, mobile, data, infrastructure, and delivery.&nbsp;You're&nbsp;comfortable challenging assumptions at any layer of the stack, and you connect technical decisions to real user and business outcomes without needing to be prompted.&nbsp;

You’ll&nbsp;bring a mix of deep technical&nbsp;expertise, strong product instincts, and collaborative leadership, acting as a trusted partner to engineering managers, product leads, and domain heads.&nbsp;

✍️Head to our&nbsp;tech blog to find out more about our engineering culture!

What&nbsp;you'll&nbsp;be doing…&nbsp;

Engineering &amp; Architecture Leadership

Lead the design of systems that are scalable, resilient, and fit for purpose, particularly&nbsp;where&nbsp;complexity cuts across team boundaries.&nbsp;

Shape the architectural direction of the&nbsp;consumer domain&nbsp;in alignment with our strategic goals.&nbsp;

Identify&nbsp;areas where we can increase reuse, improve performance, or make smarter technology decisions.&nbsp;

Delivery Impact

Partner with&nbsp;Engineering&nbsp;and&nbsp;Product Managers&nbsp;to guide scoping, sequencing, and risk mitigation for large, high-value initiatives.&nbsp;

Unblock teams by spotting better ways forward, helping shift delivery patterns or simplifying complexity.&nbsp;

Influence outcomes through strong product awareness and a focus on business and user value.&nbsp;

Standards &amp; Practices&nbsp;

Champion engineering quality, from code structure and testing practices through to observability and operational readiness.&nbsp;

Spot patterns and inefficiencies in how we build&nbsp;software,&nbsp;collaborate&nbsp;and help teams adopt better ways of working.&nbsp;

Influence how we think about platform services, shared capabilities, and long-term sustainability of our tech estate.&nbsp;

Coaching &amp; Influence&nbsp;

Be a source of mentorship and thought partnership for senior engineers and tech leads.&nbsp;

Model excellent communication, collaboration, and leadership behaviours across teams.&nbsp;

Help us grow our internal technical community, supporting better knowledge sharing and cross-domain learning.&nbsp;
