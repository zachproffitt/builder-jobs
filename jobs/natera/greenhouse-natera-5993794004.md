POSITION SUMMARY

&nbsp;

Natera is seeking a skilled, experienced and self-motivated Sr Engineering Manager to lead a software development team in a fast-growing biotech company. The team will be primarily responsible for architecting, implementing and supporting the genomics tertiary analysis software for all products across the Natera portfolio.

&nbsp;

This role includes building and leading a distributed engineering team, working close to project management and product managers, and professional communication responsibilities on high-impact, high-visibility products. The individual will also be expected to drive process improvements and represent Natera tertiary software in regulatory and client audits.

RESPONSIBILITIES:

Lead

Build and lead successful high-performing teams

Provide technical leadership for software development engineers, both onsite and remote, to ensure on-time, on-budget development of high-quality software

Own technical design, architecture and implementation of genomics tertiary analysis software supporting thousands of samples a month for new and existing product across the Natera portfolio.&nbsp;

Collaborate with leads working in other products to identify similarities and drive standardization and efficiencies

Work close with 3rd party vendors to ensure the overall solution meets the aggressive business demands including performance, scalability, cost, security and regulatory compliance, etc

Drive implementation of the best engineering practices.

Own and contribute to release planning and management for the team's software applications

Manage

Manage multiple ongoing projects.

Work closely with project managers and technical product managers to understand requirements, size development effort, assign resources, identify dependencies and agree on schedule.

Communicate issues and risks to project managers and participate in activities such as root cause analysis, lessons learned among others.

Nurture

Guide the career development of team members, nurture their technical and interpersonal skills.

Provide continuous individual performance feedback focused on recognition of achievement and opportunities for growth.

Collaborate

Build and maintain ongoing strong working relationships with stakeholders, focus on building effective communication channels.

Facilitate collaboration among cross-functional development teams, software quality engineers, and product managers.

Effect change

Provide feedback to management and initiate change to optimize software development processes with a focus on quality, innovation, and efficient product delivery.

Own process improvement initiatives including primarily software development with interfaces to software testing and product management

Grow

Learn to write and review compliant documentation per CAP, CLIA, NYS DOH, FDA, HIPAA, NIST, and other standards

Learn to represent Natera tertiary software in audits with regulatory bodies, pharmaceutical companies, and other clients

Learn software / architecture in depth with an eye toward broader responsibilities

QUALIFICATIONS:

BS or MS degree in Computer Science, Bioinformatics or equivalent demonstrated industry experience

5+ years of proven track record of building and managing high performing engineering teams&nbsp;

Extensive experience building genomics tertiary analysis software for clinical and research use

8+ years of experience in software development using Java or similar language

Experience developing software in a regulated environment

Experience in and aptitude for leading remote teams

&nbsp;

KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS, AND ABILITIES:

Proven record of managing highly effective engineering teams

Experience in building robust enterprise applications using Java or Python&nbsp;

Expertise in building highly scalable and easy to maintain distributed systems

Hands-on coding, design, architecture, and agentic AI skills&nbsp;

Strong analytical and problem-solving skills

A passion for people management and mentorship&nbsp;

Excellent verbal and written communication skills

Ability to collaborate cross-functionally and communicate effectively with reports, peers, and stakeholders&nbsp;

Demonstrate initiative with a proactive approach to addressing issues and finding creative solutions

Must demonstrate strong work ethic, work well under pressure and stressful circumstances

Experience in genomics secondary analysis solutions is a plus
