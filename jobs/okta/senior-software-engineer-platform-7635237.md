Secure Every Identity, from AI to Human

Identity is the key to unlocking the potential of AI. Okta secures AI by building the trusted, neutral infrastructure that enables organizations to safely embrace this new era. This work requires a relentless drive to solve complex challenges with real-world stakes. We are looking for builders and owners who operate with speed and urgency and execute with excellence.

This is an opportunity to do career-defining work. We're all in on this mission. If you are too, let's talk.

About the Team

Okta’s Auth0 technology is an easy-to-implement authentication and authorization platform built by developers, for developers. Every day, our platform enables 100+ million secure logins worldwide. As a Tier-0 global service, we prioritize security, privacy, and convenience so our customers can focus on innovation.

About the Role

We are seeking an experienced and motivated engineer to join our Platform Application Services team. In this role, you will design, develop, and maintain high-impact tools that enhance developer productivity, automate workflows, and reduce duplication across teams. You will collaborate with internal stakeholders to understand their needs, build scalable solutions, and ensure the reliability of our shared utilities. If you are passionate about developer experience, automation, and efficiency, we’d love to hear from you.

What You’ll Do

Develop and Maintain Scalable Tools: Build internal services and utilities that support large-scale product development.

Drive Efficiency: Identify and consolidate redundant tools to streamline engineering efforts across the organization.

Automate Workflows: Create automated processes to eliminate repetitive tasks and boost developer productivity.

Collaborate Broadly: Work closely with engineering and product teams to deliver impactful, shared solutions.

Set the Standard: Establish and enforce best practices for the development, deployment, and maintenance of internal platform services.

Ensure Reliability: Drive improvements in performance, security, and reliability, including participating in an on-call rotation for Tier-0 services.

Innovate &amp; Mentor: Explore emerging technologies to enhance our platform and provide technical guidance to team members.

What You’ll Bring

Experience: 5+ years of software development experience with a track record of building internal tools and automation.

Technical Proficiency: Strong skills in one or more languages, specifically Golang, JavaScript, TypeScript, or Node.js.

Systems Thinking: Deep understanding of software architecture, microservices, and maintainability best practices.

Automation Mindset: A proactive approach to identifying inefficiencies and solving them through code.

Operational Excellence: Experience with CI/CD pipelines, monitoring tools, and maintaining high-availability services.

Communication: Excellent documentation skills and a passion for knowledge sharing across teams.

Preferred Qualifications

Experience with gRPC and microservices architectures.

Hands-on experience with Kubernetes and containerization.

Experience deploying and managing services within AWS and/or Azure.
